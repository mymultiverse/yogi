PROJECTS




Neuroevolution for Inverted Pendulum

Implementation of the genetic algorithm to balance the inverted pendulum in a simulated cart-pole environment of OpenAI gym. An artificial neural network is used to generate control actions for given sensor data from the simulated environment. Observation data obtained from environment state used as input for neural network. Weights and biases of network updated with the evolutionary process.


Flame Dynamics in Micro Tubes

Flame characteristics changes as the flow domain reach near to quenching diameter for the flame of a particular fuel. The behaviour of H2-air premixed flame in microtubes has studied. Numerical simulations were done to observe the effect of wall thickness, tube diameter, length and convective heat transfer coefficient, velocity profiles on flame characteristics like the flame angle, flame speed and maximum temperature etc.


Project Black Mamba | Bullet Hitting Hidden Targets

Designed a supersonic bullet, capable of taking 10 degrees turn within 20 meter range. Worked on CAD, modelling and simulation. Structure analysis of low aspect ratio wing. Analytical and CFD methods used for aerodynamic and control derivative calculation under supersonic flight condition. Control surface shape optimization for maximum lift generation.


Remote Sensing and GIS for Identification of Potential Landfill

This work deals with the selection of a suitable site for the disposal of municipal solid waste generated from Shahapur Municipality (Near Mumbai) using Geographical Information System techniques with Analytical Hierarchy Process.

Modelling and Simulation of Micro Aerial Vehicle

Rigid-body dynamics with aerodynamic and sensor model analyzed. Low-level autopilot, extended Kalman filters for state-estimation, path-following routines and path-planning algorithm developed


Design and CFD Analysis of Rocket Nozzle

This project is based on comparative study and the design of 2D bell-shaped nozzles for optimal thrust performance. Numerical simulations have done using ANSYS Fluent to analyze supersonic flow in nozzles at different conditions to get optimum performance. Obtained results compared with an equivalent conical nozzle.
